"Chef Show" Hash Browns

Description

Hash browns are the perfect accompaniment to any cooked breakfast and were featured in The Chef Show. The Chef Show was inspired by the training jon Favreau received from chef Roy Choi for the movie Chef. With only four ingredients, potatoes are the star of this dish. This recipe will show you how to make them crispy and golden on the outside and perfectly cooked on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 russet potatoes, coarsely shredded
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat griddle to medium-high.
  2. Soak the potatoes in water and slosh them around to release some starch.
  3. Drain as much moisture from the shredded potatoes by squeezing in a cheesecloth or strainer, and lay on paper towels.
  4. Scatter the potatoes in an even layer on the griddle and dot with pieces of butter.
  5. Let butter melt through the potato and let sit for 4 minutes, or until the bottom starts to brown.
  6. Use bench scraper or heavy spatula to cut potatoes into even rectangles.
  7. Flip each rectangle and let sit for another 4 minutes, or until bottom starts to brown.
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste and enjoy!
