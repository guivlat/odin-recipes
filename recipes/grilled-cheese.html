"Chef" Grilled Cheese

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 slice Gruyere
  • 1 slice Parmesan
  • 1 slice yellow cheddar
  • 1 slice white cheddar
  • Softened unsalted butter, for brushing
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Brush bread slices on one side with butter.
  2. Drizzle olive oil on medium-hot griddle.
  3. Put buttered bread slices on griddle butter side down.
  4. Add cheeses on one bread slice and move bread around on griddle as cheese melts.
  5. When bread slices are golden brown, join bread.
  6. Lightly butter top of sandwich, flip, and butter the other side. Keep moving sandwich around on griddle for 4 - 5 min.
  7. Keep watch for bread color as it's easy to burn. Remove when bread is dark golden brown, BUT not burnt.
  8. Lightly butter bread with more butter and enjoy!
