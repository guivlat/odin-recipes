"Chef" Pasta Aglio e Olio

Description

Pasta aglio e olio translates to spaghetti with garlic and oil in Italian. This is another dish that Chef took and highlighted in a scene where Carl Casper (actor Jon Favreau) cooks and serves it to his restaurant hostess/girlfriend, Molly (actor Scarlett Johansson). In what can only be described as food seduction, Casper is seen moving around the kitchen while Molly watches on, with not a word exchanged between the two. This movie highlighted how transcendence can be achieved through a handful of humble ingredients turned into a bowl of pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb dried spaghetti
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 12 large garlic cloves, sliced into slivers
  • 1 tbl crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup fresh parsley, minced
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • sea salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 lemons

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of salted water to boil and add spaghetti, cooking until al dente 8-10 min.
  2. Reserve some pasta water, then drain pasta.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  4. Add garlic and stir frequently until the garlic is golden brown.
  5. Add red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  6. Add the drained spaghetti directly to the pan.
  7. Toss until spaghetti is thoroughly coated with the garlic oil, adding the reserved pasta water if necessary.
  8. Remove pan from heat. Add the parsley and Parmesan, toss well.
  9. Check for flavor and adjust the salt if necessary.
  10. Squeeze lemons to taste, garnish with more Parmesan, and enjoy!
